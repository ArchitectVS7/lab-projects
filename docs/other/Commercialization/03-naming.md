## Part 4 — Finalist Profiles

### Ritual
**Clearance: High**
No productivity or task management app using this name was found. ritual.com is a supplement brand (different category, no confusion risk). `ritual.app`, `getritual.app`, and `useRitual.com` are likely available.

> *"I wasn't sure what it was going to be at first — it sounds more like a wellness app. But once I started using it, it made complete sense. The daily check-in really is a ritual. I actually look forward to it now. The name stuck with me faster than any other tool I use."*

> *"There's something about the name that makes it feel intentional. Like this isn't a task dump — it's a practice. That framing shifted how I thought about using it."*

---

### Flowstate
**Clearance: Medium-High**
No app named Flowstate was found. "Flow"-adjacent products exist (Flowlu, Flowmo, Flow) but none claim the compound word. `flowstate.app` and `flowstate.io` are likely available. Worth a direct domain check before committing.

> *"It communicates exactly what I'm trying to get to every morning. Flow state is the goal — the app is the system that gets you there. That's a strong pitch in the name alone."*

> *"Honestly, it makes the app feel more serious than competitors. 'Flow state' is something people know and want. The name sets a bar."*

---

### Meridian
**Clearance: High**
No productivity or task management product named Meridian was found. The word appears in healthcare and finance companies but not in this software category. `meridian.app` is worth checking directly; `usemeridian.com` and `getmeridian.app` are likely clear.

> *"Meridian feels premium. I don't know why — it just does. It made me assume the product was well-designed before I even opened it. That's rare."*

> *"I like that it implies a high point — like the peak of your day. It doesn't promise you'll work harder, it promises you'll work better. That's a different message."*

---

### Stride
**Clearance: Medium**
Atlassian had a messaging product called Stride, discontinued in 2018. Some residual SEO presence remains but the product is dead and the name is not actively used. No current app conflict found. `stride.com` may be registered; `getstride.app` and `stride.so` are likely available. Verify Atlassian's trademark status before filing.

> *"Short, confident, easy to say. I told three people the name of the app I was using and they all remembered it the next time I mentioned it. That doesn't happen."*

> *"It doesn't oversell. It doesn't promise transformation. It just implies you'll keep moving. That felt honest."*
